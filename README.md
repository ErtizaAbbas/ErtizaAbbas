## 📝 Featured Theoretical Framework: The Strategic Dominance Formula

Developed across 2024–2025 and detailed in my book *The Empire* and accompanying research papers, I proposed an original analytical framework to outperform traditional material-based geopolitical models by evaluating eleven major conflicts from 1950 to 2025 (including the Korean War and the Ukraine conflict):

```math
D = (M + A) \times C^w
```

* **\(M\)**: **Mass** – Total underlying military, industrial, or economic volume metrics.
* **\(A\)**: **Alliances** – Relational power variables and coalition network structures.
* **\(C^w\)**: **Cooperation and Worldview (The Force Multiplier Exponent)** – Internal cohesion mechanics ($C$) raised to the power of the justifying narrative or belief system ($w$). 

*Statistical Application:* The model demonstrates that an entity's high internal cohesion and unifying worldview function as an exponential force multiplier, consistently neutralizing significant conventional material disadvantages.
